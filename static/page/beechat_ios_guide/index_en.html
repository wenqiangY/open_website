BeeChat iOS Download Manual

Step 1: Go to beechat.io on your mobile browser

Step 2: Tap "iOS Download" on your iOS device on the landing page

Step 3: Tap the installation button to start the download

Step 4: Tap "Install"

Step 5: Once the download has finished and you see the BeeChat app on your home screen, go to "Settings" on your iOS device and tap "General"

Step 6: Tap "Device Management"

Step 7: Tap "PHP EXPERT TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED" and then enable the developer app by tapping "Trust PHP EXPERT TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED"

Step 8: Tap "Trust"

Step 9: Open up BeeChat from your home screen and continue to the registration process
